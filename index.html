Mehmet Kaplan, Ph.D.

New Jersey Institute of Technology · University Heights, Newark, NJ 07102 · +1 (973) 596 6960 · mehmet.kaplan2@gmail.com

I work as the Associate Director of Institutional Research at New Jersey Institute of Technology. My current research focuses on formulating and conducting statistical studies addressing issues of institutional effectiveness in higher education. In addition, I am also interested in several topics related to psychometrics such as cognitive diagnosis modeling, computerized adaptive testing, and item response theory models. I obtained my M.S. degree in Statistics and Ph.D. in Educational Statistics and Measurement from Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey.
