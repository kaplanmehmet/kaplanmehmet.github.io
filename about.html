About

I am Mehmet Kaplan, a data scientist and psychometrician with experience in both academia and industry. My work sits at the intersection of measurement, statistics, and applied data science.

Previously, I worked as an assistant professor and completed a PhD in psychometrics. In recent years, I have focused on data science and business analytics roles, working with organizations to turn complex data into useful decisions.

This site collects my scholarship, professional work, and teaching and mentorship activities.
